The Best Movies According to Kaushal

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Lagaan

This is my favourite movie, plotted in the time British ruling over Indian because its related to cricket and a competition between the Indian villagers and English Men for cutting of 3 years of tax, and if villagers lose they have to pay 3 times tax.

The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind

This is a real story of a boy from an African villager where they suffer from water scarcity, due to which they cannot grow crops. In there village a tubewell is there but water is present very deep down and as there is no electricity they cannot use motor to pull out the water. Some how the boy learns about wind energy and managed to make a wind mill with his father's bicycle and it worked and they managed to supply water to the crops.

Swadesh

This is the story of an Indian guy working in American Space Agency NASA who suddenly one day think of his old care taker, in India is she well or not. Then visits her village and faces many problems in the village and manages to make a electricity generator in there village to resolve the problem of power cuts and at last leaves America and comes back to his soil India.
